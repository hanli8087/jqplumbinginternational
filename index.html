About Us

We offer a variety of specialized plumbing services so that we can service a wide variety of clients. All our work is done by qualified trades persons who are highly skilled and kept up to date on all the latest plumbing techniques and  regulations.

1

Honest and Dependable

The process from call out to quotation to invoice is completely transparent. We believe in an open door policy when it comes to our clients. All of our workers on site is under supervision and you will always be contacted before a team is dispatched to where the job or project is.

2

We Are Always Improving

Having a skilled labour force is of the utmost importance to us. We keep all our employees up to date by means of regular workshops to further improve their skills.

3

We Are registered and compliant

We are registered with the Plumbing Industry Registration Board (PIRB) as well as IOPSA. This means that we are able to provide our clients with a Certificate of Compliance once we have completed a job or project. We adhere to all industry rules and regulations and only use SABS approved material.

Services

Some of the services we offer to our clients

General Plumbing Maintenance

We can assist you with all your general plumbing maintenance. We are qualified to work on residential and commercial plumbing maintenance projects. From burst pipes to blocked pipes - we can help.

Drain/Sewer Problems?

Blocked drains/sewer lines can be a great cause for headaches and it can become a big health risk if it is not sorted out as soon as possible. Call us to come and clear the blockage out for you!

Geyser Repair/Replace

Woke up this morning and tried to take a shower and all you got was ice cold water? Call us for a quick quote to get your geyser up and running again! 

New Installations

We offer construction plumbing as well for all your new installation/renovation needs. From townhouse to hospital to schools to shopping centers. We are qualified to complete each job and project - no matter the size. 

Other Installations

We do main water line installations, new sewer line installations, water meter installations, boiler rooms, pump rooms, etc. We also erect water storage tanks  - no matter the size. 

Certificate of Compliance

We are registered and qualified, this means we are able to provide every client with a COC after we have completed a project or job. Always make sure the contract plumber you hire is registered BEFORE they start working. If they are not registered with PIRB or IOPSA, they will not be able to provide you with a Certificate of Compliance for the work they have done.
